Ariel's Point
Ariel’s Point is a popular eco-adventure destination in Boracay Island. For only 2, 500 PHP per head, you can enjoy the day trip package, which includes a boat cruise, a bbq buffet lunch, afternoon snacks, unlimited beverages (including beer and mixed drinks), and activities such as cliff diving, paddling, snorkeling, and a souvenir bracelet.

Location: Ariel's Point, Buruanga, Aklan, Philippines

Transportation: Travelers can take a car or ride a tricycle from Boracay Jetty Port to station 1 and from station 1, guests can board a boat that will give them an average 35-minute ride to arrive at Ariel’s Point.

Rates: 2,800 php per person.

Bat Caves
The Boracay Bat Cave is one of the hidden gems of Boracay. Located at the East of Yapak, Malay; the Boracay Bat Cave is well known for its crystal clear underground pools of water.

Location: East of Yapak, Malay, Aklan Province 5608 Philippines

Transportation: Tour packages can take you to the bat caves all year round, from morning to evening. And, you can go snorkeling or swimming nearby

Rates: P200 per head

Diniwid Beach
Diniwid Beach is a beautiful White Sand Beach perfect for those who seek a peaceful and quiet getaway. It is also known for its instagram-worthy Lambros Point that separates Diniwid Beach from the famous long white beach of Boracay.

Location: North of White Beach Balabag, Malay, Aklan Province 5608 Philippines

Transportation: If you want to get from White beach to Diniwid Beach. At the end of White Beach, which is just a 5-minute walking distance from Station 1 you will find Diniwid Beach.

Rates: No entrance fee.
