City Mall
The biggest supermarket on the island, CityMall. It is a modern air-conditioned shopping center. It’s located in the northern part of Boracay, just past Station 1 at the turn off to Diniwid Beach, and very close to the Fairways and Bluewater Resort.

Location:   Boracay Tambisaan Jetty Port Rd, Malay, Aklan

Business Hours:  9am until 9pm, daily

Transportation:  From Caticlan Airport, ride a tricycle to take you to Caticlan Jetty Port and pay P250 for the ferry fair. Take a taxi (usually 170PHP) that would transport you to the City Mall.

D' Mall
D’Mall is composed of 3 main areas; (1) Main Street has a large variety of shops, from tasty snack stops to souvenirs, electronics etc., (2) Palengke/ Local Market- fruits, veggies and meat, and (3) Beach Bordering Area- restaurants, souvenir shops, and clothing shops. It is very famous for tourists who want to see the delicacies of the town.

Location:   White Beach, between Station 1 and Station 2.

Business Hours:  9am until 10pm, daily

Transportation:  From Caticlan Airport, ride a tricycle to take you to Caticlan Jetty Port and pay P250 for the ferry fair. Take a taxi (usually 130PHP) that would transport you to D’Mall.

D' Talipapa
D’Talipapa is a must-visit for the seafood lover and anyone who enjoys the experience of a bustling local fish market. Known to be one of the favorite spots of tourists, not only is it famous for selling seafood, but for its “paluto” services as well.

Location:   Between White Beach and the main road, about 500 meters south from D’Mall.

Business Hours:  9am until 9pm, daily

Transportation:  From Caticlan Airport, ride a tricycle to take you to Caticlan Jetty Port and pay P250 for the ferry fair. Take a taxi (usually 110PHP) that would transport you to D’Talipapa.
