BORACAY
Boracay should be at the top of your list if you're seeking for white-sand beach resorts and magnificent coral reefs. Luxurious hotels offer international-standard lodgings, while inexpensive hotels provide a taste of the local culture. With the island's crystal-blue waters, powder-white sand, and magnificent underwater marine life, it's a modern tropical paradise with all the luxuries of a bustling metropolis. Watersports on the island include boating, diving, jet skiing, kite surfing, and windsurfing, to name a few.
